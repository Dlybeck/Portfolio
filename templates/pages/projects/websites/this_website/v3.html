DavidLybeck.com Version 3

This is the current version of the website — the one you're looking at right now. Here's how it came together:

April 22nd 2026

v3 started with throwing out the menu/tile grid from v2 and replacing it with a wall of handwritten notes pinned to a wood tabletop. Every tile is its own paper scrap — sticky notes, ripped edges, tape, different handwritten fonts and colors — so the wall reads as a real, hand-built collection instead of a generated UI.

April 23rd 2026

Opening a tile slides a cream paper rectangle in from the side. The page content lives inside it and scrolls natively. The close button turns into a back button when you've gone deeper, and tapping outside the paper closes it — one contextual button, no header chrome, no menu hamburger.

April 25th 2026

Removed the entire /dev hub and OpenHands integration that lived alongside the portfolio in v2 — now the codebase is strictly the public site. The cream paper now slides up to close (like lifting it off the table), runs larger on desktop, and gets pushed down on mobile so the navbar and close button don't overlap it.
